All Lost Prose

The songs being played in my head
Doesn’t make any sense
Neither does a poem that
breezes through my lip

songs now have been
over-exaggerated pain
and sometimes
over exaggerated happiness

the poem now has been
chewed and spat out by people
and that doesn’t make any sense

as senseless
as the world that drowns
all the songs and poem
prose now don’t feel real

it's like a child saying “she is fine”
when she lost her barbie
which she dreamed to wash
and make it clean

she couldn’t wash it
neither did she sing all prose she wrote for it
all writes did lose like how breath lost in a fog

Everything lost
A promise of a son to make his dad proud
To hug his mom, Child to wash her barbie
Poet to complete his poem

Of him to make his audience
Understand the internal meaning
Of the prose, he’s been writing
For the way too long

-Saksham Nepal
